# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## Overview
This app (aus-news) is used to automatically publish news videos to Instagram accounts owned and operated by the app developer. It does not collect, store, or share any user data.

## Data Usage
- The app accesses Instagram and Facebook Pages solely to publish video content
- No personal data from third parties is collected or processed
- No data is sold or shared with any third party

## Permissions Used
- instagram_content_publish: To post videos to Instagram
- instagram_manage_comments: To manage comments
- pages_read_engagement: To read page data
- pages_show_list: To list managed pages
